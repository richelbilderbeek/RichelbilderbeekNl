(RichelCult) Corsowijk De Korenbloem

 

In 2013 I am a voluntary welder in corsowijk De Korenbloem. They have a drawing contest. I submitted these drawings.

 

Everybody knows that to win a drawing contest, there are two main indicators that the painter is the best: (1) he/she does not cross the black border lines (2) he/she is able to paint surfaces with a very constant color. I did both of these points very well...
