Teaching

 

I teach in Dutch, English and Swedish.

 

Currently, I coordinate and teach Arduino, Blender and (game) programming at the Uppsala Makerspace, together with a team of volunteers, for students aged 8-88 years old. It's a cool course, that is both challenging and informal. You can always try it out and/or join.

 

The (Swedish) course's homepage can be found at https://bit.ly/loerdagskurser (and https://github.com/uppsala-makerspace/loerdagskurser/) Hope to see you there!
