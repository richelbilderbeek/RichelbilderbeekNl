(C++) ctime.h

 

ctime.h is an STL header file, containing the definitions of some time related functions and classes (incomplete list):

  • std::clock_t
  • std::time_t
  • std::tm
  • std::clock
  • std::difftime
  • std::mktime
  • std::time
  • std::asctime
  • std::ctime
  • std::gmtime
  • std::localtime
  • std::strftime

 

Prefer clock.h facilities over ctime.h facilities [1].
