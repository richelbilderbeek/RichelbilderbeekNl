(C++) exception.h

 

exception.h is an STL header file, containing the definitions of the exceptions in the exception hierarchy (incomplete list):

  • std::bad_alloc
  • std::bad_cast
  • std::bad_exception
  • std::bad_typeid
  • std::domain_error
  • std::exception
  • std::invalid_argument
  • std::ios_base::failure
  • std::length_error
  • std::logic_error
  • std::out_of_range
  • std::overflow_error
  • std::range_error
  • std::runtime_error
  • std::underflow_error
