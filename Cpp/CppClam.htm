(C++) CLAM

 

CLAM is a free C++ audio library.

 

CLAM is licenced under GPL 2.0 [1], so it is free. As long as the programs you develop are licenced under GPL as well, using CLAM is legal as well.

 

Under Ubuntu, CLAM cannot be installed from Ubuntu's Software Center. From the CLAM homepage, however, one can download libclam-doc_1.4.0-1_all.deb (from karmic only) to install CLAM easily.

 

I did not succeed in producing a simple beep in CLAM.
