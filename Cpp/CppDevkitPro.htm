(C++) devkitPro

 

devkitPro is the brand name for a set of toolchains to write applications and games for several of the most popular consoles.

 

  • devkitARM: GamePark GP32, Nintendo DS, Nintendo GBA
  • devkitGP2X: GamePark GP2X
  • devkitPPC: Nintendo GameCube (and maybe Nintendo Wii)
  • devkitPSP: Sony PSP
