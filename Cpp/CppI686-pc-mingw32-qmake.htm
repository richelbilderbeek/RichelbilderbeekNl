(C++) i686-pc-mingw32-qmake

 

i686-pc-mingw32-qmake is the executable used by the MXE ('MinGW X-compiling Environment') and can be used to cross-compile a Qt Creator project to a windows executable.

 

With Qt 5 installed, and when building MXE, 'make qt' does not generate i686-pc-mingw32-qmake, use 'make qtbase' additionally.

 

See MXE for examples.
