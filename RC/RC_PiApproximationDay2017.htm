(RichelCult) Pi Approximation Day 2017

 

Since 2012, I celebrate Pi Approximation Day by approximating pi with streetchalk at the Noorderplantsoen Groningen. In 2017 we were with six: B. S-B., C.IJ., J.K, M. S-B., P. S-B and me. We reached 247 digits behind the comma.

 

Our pi was filmed by the YouTube user Bhut_Trolokia, view the YouTube video here.
