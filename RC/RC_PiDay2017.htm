(RichelCult) Pi Day 2016

 

Monday it will be Pi Day, which has to be celebrated by writing pi's with streetchalk in the city center (see, for example, Pi Day 2015).

 

If you want to join:

  • When:
    • Gather: 23:57 o'clock on Sunday (a little bit more than three minutes before midnight)
    • Leave: 0:03 o'clock on Monday (a little bit more than three minutes after midnight)
    • End: when all streetchalk has been used, approximately after an hour
  • Where: De Maakplek (www.maakplek.nl , Achterweg 1, southside of the PostNl building 100 metres south of Groningen Central station)
  • What to bring: a box of street chalk, for sale in most toy stores

 

If it is raining, this event is cancelled, which will be displayed at this page:

 

Status: OK
